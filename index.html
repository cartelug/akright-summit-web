01 — The Movement

A movement with a machine behind it.

Akright Summit turns reading into competition, competition into confidence, and confidence into enterprise thinking.

Young people are flooded with shallow, fragmented content. That has weakened intellectual depth, creativity, and the long-form thinking that builds nations. The Summit is our answer — not a one-time event, but a structured journey from book, to book review, to school competition, to public pitch, to recognition.

Born from the Reading Culture Restoration Foundation — a partnership between Akright City Knowledge & Innovation Hub and Oakdale Brook Mentorship.

05 — The Framework

Think · Communicate · Alliance.

Three forces, one trajectory. The TCA framework structures every book, mentor session, and competition we run — and turns reading into enterprise.

T
Think

Develop deep thinkers, problem solvers, and visionaries. Prosperity, innovation, and transformation begin with thought.

C
Communicate

Express, refine, and share ideas through dialogue, public speaking, mentorship, and pitch — turning thought into voice.

A
Alliance

Build alliances with government, schools, corporations, mentors, and philanthropists who can open doors at scale.
