01Why Sponsor

Brand with purpose. Measurable impact.

When you sponsor Akright Summit, you are not buying a logo on a banner. You are co-authoring a movement that turns reading into entrepreneurship — and your brand into the trust signal behind it.

Every sponsor receives a personalised impact report after launch — with verified attendance, photographs, student testimonials, press clippings, and a co-branded case study you can publish to your own stakeholders.

From naming rights on the Library to mentorship hours with our top students, our tiers are designed so any organisation — local SME, regional bank, multinational, or family foundation — can find the right level of involvement.
