Five zones.
One destination.

A site transformed into a credible, attractive, and visibly serious location — one part library, one part innovation lab, one part civic meeting place. Where every program lives.

01Site Strategy

A place people drive past and then turn around for.

The current site is being transformed because it already has the rarest asset: visibility from the Kampala–Entebbe road.

Visibility creates curiosity. Curiosity creates visits. Visits create the gravity that turns a place into a destination — the kind students bring their friends to and parents bring their relatives to.

Modern finishings, considered signage, glass where it matters, generous outdoor planting, and a clean visual identity make the Hub feel inevitable — not built overnight, but built to last.

02The Five Zones
Zone 01 · Intellectual Heart

Reading & Innovation Library

The intellectual centre of the Summit. Modern, attractive, credible — a place a student is proud to study in and a parent is proud to drop them off at. Where every program begins on a page.

  • 5,000 curated books — fiction, biography, business, science, philosophy
  • Quiet study tables & individual reading nooks
  • Two glass-walled discussion rooms for small groups
  • Wi-Fi, charging, soft lighting, climate-controlled comfort
  • Open weekdays · Saturdays for Think Saturday programming
Zone 02 · Networking

The Thinkers Lounge

A comfortable space for the unhurried conversations that change directions — mentor & mentee, founder & investor, parent & head teacher, journalist & subject. Furniture you want to sit in, coffee worth talking over.

  • Soft seating arranged for two-person and four-person conversations
  • Espresso bar — open during Hub hours
  • Quiet WiFi & phone-friendly acoustics
  • Mentor Office Hours hosted here weekly
  • Pre-launch & post-event reception space
Zone 03 · The Stage

Innovation & Pitch Theatre

Flexible programmable space for talks, panels, book reviews, school pitches, judging rounds, performances, and the Grand Innovation Award finals. Stage lighting and microphone-ready, livestream-capable, photo-friendly.

  • Tiered seating for 200 (expandable to 500 with overflow)
  • Stage with backdrop, microphone, and LED screen
  • Livestream rig — every event recorded and shareable
  • Movable furniture — talk by morning, pitch by afternoon
  • Press-ready photographic vantage points
Zone 04 · After Hours

The Outdoor Social Zone

Controlled evening networking under string lights and mature trees. The reward after the serious work — music, conversation, performances, and the moments that turn contacts into friendships and friendships into collaborations.

  • Open-air lounge seating & standing networking islands
  • String lights, soft landscape lighting, fire features
  • Compact DJ booth & performance corner
  • Discrete bar & food station — quality over quantity
  • Activated for launch & quarterly Summit nights
Zone 05 · Arrival & Flow

Parking, Entry & Movement

Often forgotten, never invisible. First impressions begin in the car park and end at the gate. Smooth arrival, clear signage, easy parking, accessible entry — the kind of frictionless first ten minutes a venue is remembered for.

  • 250+ parking spots — drop-off bay for VIP, schools, and disability access
  • Clear arrival signage from the main road
  • Single curated entry experience — branded, photogenic, on-message
  • Wayfinding into each of the four other zones
  • Discreet security & visitor management — present but not heavy
