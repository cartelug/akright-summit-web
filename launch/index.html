01Chief Guest

Lt. Gen. Muhoozi Kainerugaba.

The chief guest target for the launch is Lt. Gen. Muhoozi Kainerugaba — Chief of Defence Forces of the Uganda People's Defence Forces. Confirmation in progress.

A formal invitation has been drafted, with backup chief-guest options in motion in parallel. We are pursuing the right voice for the right movement — one that signals seriousness, supports youth ambition, and brings the country's attention to a reading and innovation culture worth building.

Confirmed remarks & speaker list will be published 14 days before launch.

Programme Note

"The event must look like the beginning of a serious movement: books, thinkers, job creators, dreamers, and a world-class bookshop / innovation location."

Launch Brief · RCRF

05Who Is Coming

A room of
eight audiences.

Carefully invited — every seat in the room earned. The launch audience is a deliberate cross-section of the people who will carry the movement forward.

Students

Top performers from the 36 first-wave partner schools — the protagonists of the movement.

Schools

Head teachers, deans, and school coordinators — the channel through which we reach every student.

Parents

Family members of the showcase students — the supporters who make the journey possible.

Universities

Vice chancellors, deans, and academic leaders — the next step on the student journey.

Corporates

Sponsors, CSR leads, talent partners — businesses backing the next generation of builders.

Government

Ministers, agencies, ambassadors — the public partners who can scale what works.

Media & Press

National and regional outlets, photographers, livestream producers — the people who carry the story.

Mentors

Founders, executives, academics, professionals — those willing to spend an hour a month on a young thinker.

Public & Friends

The broader audience who simply want to be in the room when a movement starts. Capacity-permitting.
